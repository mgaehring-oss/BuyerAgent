# Vendor Research & Scoring Guide

Reference this guide when evaluating chemical suppliers.

---

## Ranking Rules (from CLAUDE.md)

1. **Longevity** — A vendor in business longer ranks higher. A company founded in 1980 outranks one founded in 2010, all else being equal.
2. **US preference** — Prefer US-based suppliers unless the international price is more than 10% cheaper (after accounting for shipping and duties).
3. **Drop shipping** — Required capability. Vendors that cannot drop ship should be ranked lower or excluded.

---

## Scoring Criteria (use when comparing vendors)

| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Years in business | High | Check "About Us" / LinkedIn / DUNS |
| US-based headquarters | High | Faster shipping, easier returns, no import duties |
| Drop shipping available | High | Must confirm explicitly |
| Price competitiveness | Medium | Compare per-unit price at requested quantity |
| Quality certifications | Medium | ACS, USP, NF, cGMP, ISO 9001, COA availability |
| Minimum order quantity (MOQ) | Medium | Lower MOQ = more flexible for client |
| Catalog breadth | Low | Broad catalog suggests established distributor |
| Website professionalism | Low | Indicator of legitimacy; check for SSL, contact info |

## Recommended Search Strategies

### Primary search
```
"[chemical name]" supplier "drop ship" site:.com
"[chemical name]" buy wholesale USA laboratory supplier
```

### Verifying establishment date
- Check company "About Us" page
- Search: `"[company name]" founded history`
- LinkedIn company profile → "Founded" field
- Better Business Bureau listing

### Verifying drop shipping
- Look for "Drop Ship Program", "Reseller Program", or "Wholesale" pages
- Search: `"[company name]" drop ship reseller program`
- If not listed publicly, note as "Confirm drop ship availability"

### Verifying US location
- Physical address on website (not PO Box)
- Check DUNS / SAM.gov registration for distributors
- "About Us" or "Contact" page showing US address

## Red Flags — Vendors to Avoid or Scrutinize

- No physical US address listed
- Founded less than 3 years ago with no parent company
- No certificate of analysis (COA) available
- No SSL on website (http:// only)
- Prices dramatically below market (may indicate quality issues)
- No business phone number listed

## Justification Writing Guide

Your 2–3 sentence justification for the #1 vendor should address:
1. **Why this vendor over others** (longevity, location, certifications)
2. **Price/value** (competitive pricing, volume discounts, drop ship terms)
3. **Risk mitigation** (established reputation, quality guarantees)

**Example:**
> "Spectrum Chemical, founded in 1971 and headquartered in Gardena, CA, is the top choice due to its 50+ years of US operations and confirmed drop shipping program for resellers. Their ACS reagent grade product is priced at $X/kg — within 3% of the lowest quote — and they provide a full certificate of analysis with each shipment. Their long-standing reputation in the specialty chemicals market and domestic supply chain minimize fulfillment risk."
